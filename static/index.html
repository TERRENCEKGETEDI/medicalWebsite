General Practitioner(GP)

Role: Often the first point of contact for patients, handling a broad range of health issues and referring to specialists as needed.

Cardiologist

Role: Treat heart-related issues, often consulted for chest pain, heart attacks, and other cardiovascular emergencies.

Orthopedic Surgeon

Role: Treat musculoskeletal injuries such as fractures, dislocations, and joint problems, perform surgeries.

Radiologist

Role: Interpret imaging studies (e.g., X-rays, CT scans, MRIs) to diagnose and monitor conditions

Pediatrician

Role: Provide medical care for children, often involved in emergency situations involving pediatric patients.

Gynecologist / Obstetrician (OB/GYN)

Role: Manage childbirth and related complications, treat female reproductive system issues.

Neurologist

Role: Treat conditions related to the nervous system, such as strokes, seizures, and severe headaches.

Oncologist

Role: Treat cancer patients, often consulted for diagnosis and management of malignancies.

Psychiatrist

Role: Manage mental health emergencies, such as severe depression, anxiety, and psychosis

Anesthesiologist

Role: Administer anesthesia and manage pain before, during, and after surgeries..

Pulmonologist

Role: Treat respiratory system issues, often consulted for pneumonia, asthma, and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).

Endocrinologist

Duties: Treat hormone-related disorders (e.g., diabetes, thyroid disorders, adrenal issues), manage metabolic diseases. Specialization: Endocrine system (hormones).

Urologist

Duties: Treat urinary tract issues (e.g., infections, kidney stones), manage male reproductive health issues. Specialization: Urinary system and male reproductive organs.

Ngaka ya Kakaretšo(GP) .

Karolo: Gantši ntlha ya mathomo ya kgokagano ya balwetši, go swaragana le ditaba tše di fapafapanego tša maphelo le go šupa ditsebi ge go nyakega.

Ngaka ya pelo

Karolo: Alafa ditaba tšeo di tswalanago le pelo, gantši di boledišanwa bakeng sa bohloko bja sehuba, ditlhaselo tša pelo le maemo a mangwe a tšhoganetšo a pelo le methapo.

Ngaka ya go Bua marapo

Karolo: Alafa dikgobalo tša mešifa le marapo a go swana le go robega, go phatloga le mathata a manonyeletšo, dira go buiwa.

Setsebi sa tša mahlasedi

Karolo: Hlatholla dithuto tša go tšea diswantšho (mohlala, X-ray, CT scan, MRI) go hlahloba le go hlokomela maemo

Ngaka ya bana

Karolo: Nea tlhokomelo ya tša kalafo bakeng sa bana, gantši ba akaretšwa maemong a tšhoganetšo ao a akaretšago balwetši ba bana.

Ngaka ya basadi / Ngaka ya pelego (OB/GYN) .

Karolo: Laola pelego le mathata ao a amanago le ona, alafa ditaba tša tshepedišo ya pelego ya basadi.

Ngaka ya tša methapo

Karolo: Alafa maemo ao a tswalanago le tshepedišo ya megalatšhika, a bjalo ka go hwa lehlakore, go thothomela le go opša ke hlogo o šoro.

Ngaka ya kankere

Karolo: Alafa balwetši ba kankere, gantši ba rerišana bakeng sa go hlahloba le go laola malwetši a kotsi.

Ngaka ya tša monagano

Karolo: Laola maemo a tšhoganetšo a tša maphelo a monagano, a bjalo ka kgateletšego e šoro, go tshwenyega le bolwetši bja monagano

Ngaka ya go idibatša

Karolo: Efa anesthesia le go laola bohloko pele, nakong le ka morago ga go buiwa

Ngaka ya maswafo

Karolo: Alafa ditaba tša tshepedišo ya go hema, gantši di boledišanwa bakeng sa nyumonia, asthma le bolwetši bjo bo sa folego bja go šitiša maswafo (COPD).

Setsebi sa tša endocrinology

Mešomo: Alafa malwetši ao a amanago le dihomoune (mohlala, bolwetši bja swikiri, malwetši a thyroid, ditaba tša adrenal), laola malwetši a metaboliki. Specialization: Tshepedišo ya endocrine (dihomoune).

Ngaka ya urologist

Mešomo: Alafa ditaba tša pampišana ya moroto (mohlala, ditwatši, maswika a dipshio), laola ditaba tša maphelo a pelego a banna. Specialization: Tshepedišo ya moroto le ditho tša pelego tša banna.
